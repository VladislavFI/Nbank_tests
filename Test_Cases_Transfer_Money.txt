1. Перевод денег с одного аккаунта на другой

curl -X 'POST' \
  'http://localhost:4111/api/v1/accounts/transfer' \
  -H 'accept: */*' \
  -H 'Authorization: Basic a2F0ZTE5OTg3Nzp2ZXJ5c1RSb25nUGFzc3dvcmQzMyQ=' \
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  -d '{
  "senderAccountId": 1,
  "receiverAccountId": 2,
  "amount": 50
}'
